Scheme Name:Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan (PM-KUSUM)

State/Central:Central Government Scheme,Implemented jointly by Central + State Governments

Target Audience:Farmers and rural landowners, including:
Small and marginal farmers
Farmers using irrigation pumps
Farmers with barren/unused land (for solar plants)

Also benefits:
Farmer cooperatives
Panchayats
Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs)

Objective:
To promote solar energy in agriculture and reduce dependence on diesel/electric pumps
To increase farmers’ income by allowing them to generate and sell solar power

Eligibility Criteria:
Land Ownership Farmers can: Install solar pumps, Set up solar power plants on barren or cultivable land

Age Limit : Not specifically mentioned in the official guidelines PDF

Citizenship : Must be an Indian farmer / beneficiary (implied under Government of India scheme)

Exclusions (Who CANNOT apply):
Non-farmers or entities without land access
Projects not meeting MNRE technical guidelines
Delayed or non-compliant installations may lose benefits/subsidy

Financial Benefits:
Subsidy support (CFA – Central Financial Assistance) provided by Government
Funds released in stages:
Up to 40% advance
Additional 30% after progress
Remaining after completion

Additional Benefits
Farmers can:
Replace diesel pumps with solar pumps
Solarize grid-connected pumps
Sell surplus electricity to DISCOMs (extra income)

Required Documents:

(Not listed as a fixed checklist in PDF, but required based on implementation process)

Aadhaar / Identity proof
Land ownership or usage proof
Bank account details
Project/application documents (through implementing agency)
