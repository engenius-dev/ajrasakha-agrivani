# Scheme Name: Direct Seeded Rice (DSR) Incentive Scheme
**State/Central:** Punjab State Government

**Target Audience:** Paddy farmers, especially small and marginal farmers

## 1. Objective
To promote Direct Seeded Rice (DSR) technology in place of traditional transplanting in order to conserve groundwater, reduce cultivation costs, and encourage sustainable agriculture.

## 2. Eligibility Criteria
* **Land Ownership:** Must be cultivating paddy on eligible agricultural land in Punjab
* **Age Limit:** Not specifically defined
* **Crop Type:** Applicable only for paddy (rice) cultivation using DSR method
* **Citizenship:** Must be a resident farmer of Punjab (India)

## 3. Exclusions (Who CANNOT apply)
* Farmers not adopting the DSR method
* Farmers not registered under the scheme
* Land not suitable or not verified for paddy cultivation
* Institutional or non-agricultural landholders

## 4. Financial Benefits
* ₹1,500 per acre incentive for adopting DSR
* Additional support: Subsidy on DSR machinery/equipment, Technical assistance from agriculture department

## 5. Required Documents
* Aadhaar Card
* Land ownership records (Jamabandi / Patta)
* Bank account details (for DBT)
* Crop sowing details / registration proof
* Mobile number linked with Aadhaar
