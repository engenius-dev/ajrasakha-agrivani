<h1>Scheme Name: Direct Seeded Rice (DSR) Incentive Scheme</h1>
<h4>State/Central:</h4> Punjab State Government
<h4>Target Audience:</h4> Paddy farmers, especially small and marginal farmers
<h4>1. Objective</h4>
To promote Direct Seeded Rice (DSR) technology in place of traditional transplanting in order to conserve groundwater, reduce cultivation costs, and encourage sustainable agriculture.
<h4>2. Eligibility Criteria</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Land Ownership: Must be cultivating paddy on eligible agricultural land in Punjab</li>
  <li>Age Limit: Not specifically defined</li>
  <li>Crop Type: Applicable only for paddy (rice) cultivation using DSR method</li>
  <li>Citizenship: Must be a resident farmer of Punjab (India)</li>
  <li>Citizenship: Must be a resident farmer of Punjab (India)</li>
</ul>
<h4>3. Exclusions (Who CANNOT apply)</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Farmers not adopting the DSR method</li>
  <li>Farmers not registered under the scheme</li>
  <li>Land not suitable or not verified for paddy cultivation</li>
  <li>Institutional or non-agricultural landholders</li>
</ul>
<h4>4. Financial Benefits</h4>
<ul>
  <li>₹1,500 per acre incentive for adopting DSR</li>
  <li>Additional support: Subsidy on DSR machinery/equipment , Technical assistance from agriculture department</li>
</ul>
<h4>5. Required Documents</h4>
<ul>
 <li>Aadhaar Card</li>
 <li>Land ownership records (Jamabandi / Patta)</li>
 <li>Bank account details (for DBT)</li>
 <li>Crop sowing details / registration proof</li>
 <li>Mobile number linked with Aadhaar</li>
</ul>
