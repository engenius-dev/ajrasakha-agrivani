# Scheme Name: Kalaignar All Village Integrated Agriculture Development Programme (KAVIADP)
**State/Central:** Tamil Nadu State Government

**Target Audience:** Small and marginal farmers, farmer groups, rural households

## 1. Objective
To achieve integrated agricultural development at the village level by enhancing productivity, improving infrastructure, and promoting allied agricultural activities such as horticulture and livestock, thereby increasing farmer income and ensuring sustainable rural development.

## 2. Eligibility Criteria
* **Land Ownership:** Farmers owning or cultivating agricultural land in selected villages.
* **Age Limit:** Not specifically defined.
* **Crop Type:** Applicable to all crops, horticulture, and allied activities (livestock, fisheries, etc.).
* **Citizenship:** Must be a resident farmer of Tamil Nadu.
* **Additional Requirement:** Village must be selected under the scheme implementation list.

## 3. Exclusions (Who CANNOT apply)
* Farmers from non-selected villages under KAVIADP.
* Individuals not engaged in agricultural or allied activities.
* Institutional or non-agricultural beneficiaries.

## 4. Financial Benefits
* Financial assistance for crop cultivation inputs, horticulture and plantation development, livestock, and allied activities.
* Infrastructure support for irrigation facilities, storage, and rural infrastructure.
* Subsidies for farm equipment and development activities.

## 5. Required Documents
* Aadhaar Card
* Land ownership records (Patta/Chitta)
* Bank account details (for DBT)
* Residence proof
* Farmer registration details (if applicable)
