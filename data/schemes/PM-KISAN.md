# Scheme Name: Pradhan Mantri KIsan SAmman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
**State/Central:** Central Government (Central Sector Scheme)
**Target Audience:** Eligible farmers (The PM-KISAN portal is designed only for a single central sector scheme. The scheme provides ₹6,000 per year to eligible farmers in 3 installments.)

## 1. Objective
To provide income support to farmers, reduce their financial burden, and ensure Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).

## 2. Eligibility Criteria
* **Land Ownership:** Must be a Small and Marginal landholder farmer family. The family must collectively own cultivable land up to 2 hectares as per the land records of the concerned State/UT. The existing land-ownership system is used for the identification of beneficiaries.
* **Family Definition:** A family is officially defined as comprising a husband, wife, and minor children.
* **Age Limit:** No specific age limit is mentioned in the scheme, but the beneficiary must fit within the family definition above.
* **Crop Type:** No restriction on crop type. The scheme applies to all types of cultivable land, regardless of crops grown.
* **Citizenship:** Must be an Indian citizen. Must belong to a farmer family owning cultivable land in India.

## 3. Exclusions (Who CANNOT apply)
* All Institutional Land Holders.
* Former and present Ministers, MPs, MLAs, Mayors, and Chairpersons of District Panchayats.
* Serving or retired officers and employees of Central/State Government Ministries.
* All retired pensioners whose monthly pension is ₹10,000 or more.
* Professionals (High-income category).
* Income tax payers.
* Former and present holders of constitutional posts.

## 4. Financial Benefits
* ₹6,000 per year per eligible farmer family.
* Paid in 3 equal installments: ₹2,000 each.
* Given every 4 months.
* Payments are made every year, subject to eligibility verification.
* If a property is owned by multiple people (joint holding), the benefit goes to the family member who owns the most land.

## 5. Required Documents
* **Identity Proof (Mandatory):** Aadhaar Number (preferred). If Aadhaar is not available: Aadhaar Enrollment Number OR any valid ID such as Voter ID, Driving License, NREGA Job Card, or other government-issued ID.
* **Bank Account Details:** Bank Account Number and IFSC Code. Account must be in the beneficiary’s name and is used for Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).
* **Mobile Number:** Required for SMS alerts and payment updates (Optional but recommended).
* **Land Records:** Proof of ownership of cultivable land. Verified through State/UT land records system.
* **Personal Details:** Name, Gender, Category (SC/ST, etc.), Address, Age/Date of Birth (optional).
* **Self-Declaration:** Declaration confirming eligibility and not falling under exclusion categories. Used by State/UT for verification.
